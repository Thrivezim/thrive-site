Acid Reflux

Fire Cider (also known as Master Tonic) and vinegars like Apple Cider Vinegar help stimulate stomach acid and digestion. Slowly weaning off acid-blocking medications is essential — these drugs damage gut health and make your system vulnerable to **H. pylori**, a bacteria linked to ulcers and poor digestion.
